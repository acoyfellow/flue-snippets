# Refunds — Customer Support Knowledge Base

## When to issue a refund

We issue full refunds within 30 days of purchase, no questions asked.
After 30 days, refunds require manager approval.

## Required information

To process a refund, ask the customer for:

- The order number (format: `ORD-XXXXXX`)
- The reason for the refund
- Whether they want store credit instead

## Common refund reasons

- **Changed mind** — full refund, no questions
- **Defective product** — full refund + free return shipping
- **Wrong item shipped** — full refund + free return shipping + 10% off next order
- **Late delivery** — full refund OR keep the item + 25% credit
